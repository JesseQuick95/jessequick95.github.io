Design

Computer Aided Design (CAD) and 3D modelling of mechanical components and system assemblies.

I have had experience with a variety of CAD software including SolidWorks, AutoDesk Inventor and ANSYS SpaceClaim.

Thermal energy storage tank design

I worked on the design of a thermal energy storage tank for a utility scale power plant as my final year design project. The design uses phase change materials to store heat that can be used to generate electricity when there is no sunlight available.

Each component was designed and assembled in AutoDesk Inventor. The design was modular and could scaled for the storage needs of the solar power station. The final design sored molten salt at 3500C and provided energy for 8 hours for a 4.6MW power plant.

Parametric and automated design

This project created an automated parametric design in ANSYS SpaceClaim for an impinging jet absorber with fins. The entire design can be changed and analysed for more than 10 design parameters using Python scripting in geometry model. This is extremely useful for optimization of the receiver to increase heat transfer and reduce the pressure losses.
